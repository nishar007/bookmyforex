BookMyForex Forex Card Security Features

Chip & Pin Security

  • POS and ATM payments are secured with an EMV (Europay MasterCard Visa) chip, offering better protection than magnetic stripe cards, which are vulnerable to skimming.

  • Chip cards are harder to clone and won't authorize transactions via the magnetic stripe, prompting chip insertion instead.

  • For added security, the chip stays in the machine while the user enters their PIN to complete the transaction.

Disposable Virtual Cards

  • Disposable virtual cards add security to international e-commerce payments by preventing unauthorized charges from foreign merchants, who can bypass the 3DS OTP step required by Indian regulations.

  • These temporary cards can be created, accessed, and discarded directly through the BookMyForex app, ensuring your physical card details remain protected.

  • Simply generate a virtual card for each overseas transaction, use its details at checkout, and dispose of it afterward, safeguarding your physical card from future misuse.

Insurance Cover

  • Despite all security measures, if things still go wrong, you can always raise a chargeback/dispute a transaction or use our free-of-cost insurance cover, provided by HDFC Ergo.

  • Our insurance cover protects you from all fraudulent or unauthorized transactions that may be executed resulting from card counterfeiting, skimming, phishing, and internet banking fraud or loss of your card.

BookMyForex App Security Features

Bank Grade Security Infrastructure & Tools

  • We protect data in transit and at rest using the strongest encryption and hashing algorithms.

  • Our servers are secured behind firewalls in SOC2-certified cloud data centers across India, with sensitive information either hashed or double-encrypted using AES encryption and regular key rotation.

  • All traffic to our servers is encrypted via HTTPS and SSL, safeguarded by web application firewalls (WAF), edge detection tools, and real-time monitoring with CSPM/DSPM tools to prevent threats.

Regular Security Audits & Testing

  • Our website, applications, and infrastructure go through stringent vulnerability assessment and penetration testing (VAPT) audits conducted by CERT-IN empaneled security vendors at least twice per year.

  • Our application's code is also audited to ensure compliance with secure coding guidelines.

  • Additionally, we run bug bounty programs and invest in people security management training to create a watertight security posture.

Strong Authentication & Authorisation

  • All our backend applications are protected using multi-factor authentication and strong access control list (ACL) based permissions.

  • On the customer side, we use strong biometric, password, and OTP-based authentication along with other security measures to safeguard and restrict unauthorized access to your account.

Forex Card Security FAQs

How secure is the BookMyForex card for international transactions?

BookMyForex cards are highly secure with chip and PIN protection, 3D secure authentication for online transactions, and real-time transaction alerts. They comply with international security standards and offer 24/7 fraud monitoring.

What should I do if my forex card is lost or stolen?

Immediately block your card through the BookMyForex app or by calling our 24/7 customer service. You can request a replacement card, and in an emergency, you can also access your funds through emergency cash withdrawal services.

Are there any security features in the BookMyForex app?

Yes, the BookMyForex app includes biometric authentication, two-factor authentication, and the ability to instantly freeze/unfreeze your card. You can also set transaction limits and receive real-time notifications for all card activities.

Can someone use my forex card if they don't know the PIN?

Without the PIN, in-person transactions are not possible. However, some online transactions might be processed without a PIN. This is why we recommend keeping your card details secure and using the app to lock your card when not in use.

How does the disposable virtual card feature work?

Disposable virtual cards generate a temporary card number for one-time online use. After the transaction, these details become invalid, protecting your actual card information from potential breaches during online shopping.

What insurance coverage comes with the BookMyForex card?

Our forex cards include insurance against fraudulent transactions, loss or theft protection, and emergency assistance. The coverage varies by card type, with premium cards offering higher protection limits and additional travel insurance benefits.

Is it safe to use my forex card on public Wi-Fi networks?

We recommend avoiding transactions on public Wi-Fi. If necessary, use a VPN service for an added layer of security. Alternatively, use mobile data or trusted networks when managing your card or making online purchases.

How do I set up transaction alerts for my forex card?

Transaction alerts are automatically enabled for all customers. You'll receive notifications via SMS and email. You can customize alert preferences in the BookMyForex app under "Card Settings" > "Notification Preferences".

Can I use the BookMyForex card at all international ATMs?

BookMyForex cards can be used at ATMs displaying the Visa or Mastercard logo worldwide. For security reasons, we recommend using ATMs attached to banks and checking the machine for any suspicious devices before inserting your card.

What security measures are in place for contactless transactions?

Contactless transactions have built-in security limits, typically under $50. For higher amounts, PIN verification is required. You can disable contactless functionality through the app if you prefer, and all contactless transactions are covered by our fraud protection.
